Eric Scarbrough for Superior Court Judge

Orange County, CA

Greetings,

My name is Eric Scarbrough and I am running for Orange County Superior Court Judge. Welcome to my website and thank you for taking the time to learn more about me.

The role of judge carries tremendous responsibility. It requires not only legal knowledge, but also integrity, humility, an open mind, hard work, and commitment to the highest ethical standards. I want the opportunity to serve my community as a judge who pursues justice with compassion and understanding.

I have been a prosecutor with the Orange County District Attorney’s Office since 2004. For nearly two decades, I have strived to help and protect all of the people and neighborhoods of Orange County. For more than a dozen years, I have specialized in prosecutions of murders and cases involving sexual assaults against adults and children. I have prosecuted child molesters, rapists, murderers, and serial killers.

Now more than ever, as people from all walks of life are losing faith in our justice system, it is important to have someone who is hard working, ethical, and committed to justice for all people. My background and upbringing provide perspective which complement the experiences of my legal career. They have shaped me into a person who can fulfill the obligations and responsibilities of a judicial officer, foster greater trust of the legal system and serve all people of Orange County.

I’m asking for your support and your vote on June 7, 2022.
